WCA

The World Cube Association (WCA) is the governing body for competitive Rubik's Cube and other twisty puzzle events worldwide. The WCA organizes and sanctions competitions for various puzzle sizes, from 2x2 to 7x7 cubes. In these competitions, speedcubers aim to solve these puzzles as quickly as possible, often employing advanced solving methods and algorithms to achieve record-breaking times. In addition to standard cubes, the WCA also recognizes a diverse range of other puzzle events, such as Clock, Megaminx, Pyraminx, Skewb, and Square-1, where participants compete to solve these unique puzzles with speed and precision. These events foster a global community of puzzle enthusiasts who continually push the boundaries of human dexterity and problem-solving skills.
